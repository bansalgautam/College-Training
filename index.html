OUR VISION

Our vision is to help the poor children right out there by providing them free education, then train them the skills they are intersted in so that they can earn money and support their families. We have served more then 400 children now. We also provide authors a platform to get their books published and also help them marketing their books. We are working on creating the biggest e-library to help lakhs of students across the globe, all this either at minimal cost or absolutely free. Most of them being absolutely free.
